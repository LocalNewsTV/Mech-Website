Team AutoBee

Introduction

Team Autobee's project is to make a beeswax food wrap cutting machine for Abeego. Abeego is a local company that mainly produces reusable food wrap with 100% zero waste. The company highly focuses on sustainability and is certified as a B corporation.

Project

At the end of April, our team met with Toni Desrosiers, the founder of Abeego. Toni sought a machine to reduce manual labor and work strain in her production facility. Our team was immediately excited by the potential of the project and the vision expressed by Toni. Our machine removes the manual cutting process by automating the trimming and cutting process of the food wrap.

The machine utilizes commercial-off-the-shelf (COTS) components and in-house design parts. Our team places ease of use, longevity, and manufacturability as the top design priorities. The machinery consists of a feed system, trim system, length-cutting system, end-product collection system, and control system. All systems are integrated to create a functional machine.
