The Konditioner Kings

Introduction

The Konditioner Kings have partnered with the founder of Green Room, Danielle Lapointe, to create a multipurpose conditioner-bar cutter. The mechanism will be used, exclusively, by the Green Room company who specializes in vegan, plastic-free, biodegradable shampoo and conditioner bars.

Project

For this project, we are designing a multipurpose cutter that is able to cut off the meniscus as well as cut bars to a desired size. The meniscus is an undesirable, U-shaped feature, that develops on the top of a liquid when the containerâ€™s material attracts the molecules of the liquid. Our design will be provided in a compact, lightweight, and efficient manner while remaining cost-effective.
