Fork to Ball Design

Introduction

Fork to Ball Design (FTB) is a group that designs and manufactures trailers capable of lifting pallets. We design our products from fork to ball. Holocene distillery is a company owned and operated by Dorian Redden. His distillery makes gin and absinthe using locally sourced ingredients. Holocene distillery relies on heavy loads of grain to be delivered to its facility to manufacture its products.

Project

FTB Design is manufacturing a solution for Holocene distillery to safely transport loads of grain up a hill to their facility. We are proposing a pallet jack trailer, which can be towed behind a vehicle, to solve this problem.
