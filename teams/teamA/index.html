Dionysus Systems

Introduction

Dionysus systems is excited to be designing and manufacturing a mash press for Holocene Distilling Project. Our product will assist our client, Dorian Redden, in achieving his goal of carbon neutrality by allowing him to recover otherwise lost or wasted energy stored in the mash as fuel for his boiler.

Project

The primary waste product from distilling operations is called mash, an organic matter from which starches and sugars have been removed. Mash has a high-moisture content, which must be removed quickly after distilling to prevent spoilage. Our team will design an apparatus that can remove large amounts of water from the mash to increase storage time until it can be further processed into pellets.
