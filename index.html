About

Each year, students of Mechanical Engineering Technology work together in teams to create client-sponsored mechanical projects. Students take their project through phases of concept, design, prototyping, manufacturing, assembly and troubleshooting and showcase their projects to the public and various tech professionals. Join us at Camosun College Interurban Campus Center for Trades Education & Innovation on August 11.
